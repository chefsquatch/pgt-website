About

An independent software company.

Precision Guesswork Technologies is an independent software company based in Flint, Michigan, founded by Les Fleming — Executive Chef, systems thinker, and software architect.

We build local-first, privacy-first software and mobile applications. Every product starts from the gap: we find a real human problem and build the technology to solve it — no more, no less.

The name carries forward the founder’s late father’s race team, Precision Guesswork Racing. The standard is the same one earned over 22 years in professional kitchens: nothing leaves the pass until it’s right.
